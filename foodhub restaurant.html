Our Heritage

Rooted in Rajkot,
Born to Nourish

Founded in 2009 on the busy streets of Yagnik Road, Food Hub began as a humble family kitchen. Today it stands as one of Rajkot's most cherished dining institutions — a place where the soul of Gujarati cooking lives on in every pot, every tawa, every bite.

We grind our own spices each morning, source seasonal vegetables from local farms in Saurashtra, and cook each dish to order — because real flavour cannot be rushed. Jain-friendly and no-onion/garlic options are always available.
